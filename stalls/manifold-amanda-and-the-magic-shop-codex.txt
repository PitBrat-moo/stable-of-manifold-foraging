  The sequence begins with a threshold event. Amanda discovers
  an impossible doorway and enters before understanding. The
  crossing establishes the primary geometry: commitment precedes
  orientation.

  Doctor Watt introduces a world in which objects are read as
  biographies rather than possessions. Through the shop Amanda
  encounters wonder as a diagnostic instrument. Delight reveals
  the regions of life she has stopped inhabiting.

  The satchel emerges as the compression object of the narrative.
  It embodies GOOD, WORN, and COMFORTABLE simultaneously. Its

  inviting her own contribution.

  changes because she keeps moving.

  The object is not retained indefinitely. It is released at the
  moment the relationship reaches completion. The satchel's record
  survives precisely because it continues elsewhere.
