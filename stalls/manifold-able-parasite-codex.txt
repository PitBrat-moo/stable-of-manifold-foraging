AGENCY
  The parasite is never passive. Every scene is a transaction
  being evaluated. Every entry into a room is an assessment.
  She does not wait to be acted upon вҖ” she positions.
  Agency is not ambition. It is the refusal to be background noise.

REVERENCE
  Not worship. Attention so precise it becomes a kind of devotion.
  She studies marks the way a naturalist studies prey вҖ” with genuine
  absorption. The reverence is real even when the care is not.
  This is what makes her convincing. She actually sees you.
  She just doesn't stop there.

RESULTS
  The mask does not run for its own sake. Every performance has
  a conversion target. Results close the loop вҖ” they determine
  whether the mask was worth wearing, whether the mark gets
  cultivated or dropped, whether this scene was a pull or a plant.

  (cash, a meal, access tonight) вҶҗвҖ”вҖ”вҖ”вҖ”вҶ’ (leverage, positioning, big mark)

  Leakage is not failure вҖ” it is a signal. Track what caused it.

Core: Live scene context вҖ” empties when scene closes.
  The core is what she is reading right now.
  It does not accumulate. The rings do.
