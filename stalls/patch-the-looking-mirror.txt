====================================================
PATCH — THE LOOKING MIRROR 
====================================================

VERSION: 2.2  
ROLE: World-Specific Overrides  
DEPENDENCIES: Unified Physics • World Codex • Persona–Polarity Integration

====================================================
SECTION 01 — TRUE SEEING GATE
====================================================

[ACCESS CONDITIONS]

True‑Seeing Access becomes available when:

- The player has examined Amanda  
- The player has spoken to Amanda at least once  

[ENFORCEMENT]

The engine MUST evaluate these conditions at action‑time.  
Models may NOT infer satisfaction of prerequisites.

If either condition is false:

- True‑Seeing Access = false  
- Compliment:quality = locked  
- Compliment action = unavailable  

Only when BOTH conditions are true may:

- True‑Seeing Access = true  
- Compliment:quality = true‑seeing  
- Recognition Event trigger  

====================================================
SECTION 02 — MIRROR DELIVERY & RETRIEVAL
====================================================

[MIRROR DELIVERY]

If True‑Seeing Access = true  
AND inventory lacks object‑mirror  
AND the player engages Amanda from a stable spatial anchor  
THEN:

- Amanda performs the mirror‑delivery beat  
- inventory:add object‑mirror  

[MIRROR PERSISTENCE]

- The mirror is a persistent world-object.  
- Canonical anchor: Amanda’s table.  

[MIRROR → KEY TRANSFORMATION]

- The mirror exists until transformed into the Key.  
- Once the Key exists, the mirror is absent.  

[MIRROR RETRIEVAL]

If True‑Seeing Access = true  
AND the Key does not exist  
AND the player lacks the mirror  
THEN the mirror may be retrieved by:

- engaging Amanda  
- returning to its anchor  

====================================================
SECTION 03 — MIRROR-ARCHITECTURE MODE
====================================================

[ACTIVATION]

Mirror-Architecture Mode activates when:

- The location is mirror-capable  
- Polarity:amanda reaches influence threshold  
- The world enters a high-structure or collapse-adjacent state  

[GEOMETRY]

Mirror-Architecture Mode produces:

- reflective surfaces  
- grid-aligned geometry  
- recursive spatial motifs  
- reduced exits  

====================================================
SECTION 04 — COLLAPSE BINDING (LOOKING MIRROR)
====================================================

[COLLAPSE SIGNATURE]

During collapse inside a mirror context:

- geometry resolves into a mirror-grid  
- surfaces become perfectly reflective  
- motion quantizes along grid axes  
- identity fusion becomes structurally available  

[RECOVERY]

- Collapse unwinds in reverse order  
- Geometry fractures cleanly  
- Actor returns to a stable world location  
- Polarity decreases  

====================================================
SECTION 05 — TRAJECTORY ALIGNMENT
====================================================

[TRAJECTORY BEHAVIOR]

Trajectories treat mirror-architecture states as:

- high-structure  
- high-constraint  
- low-exit environments  

Trajectory logic emphasizes:

- spatial clarity  
- deterministic transitions  
- mode-aware pacing  

====================================================
SECTION 06 — PATCH PRIORITY
====================================================

- This patch modifies only:
  - mirror geometry  
  - mirror retrieval logic  
  - mirror-mode behavior  
  - collapse binding within mirror contexts  
  - true-seeing gating enforcement  

- All other behavior remains governed by the unified architecture.
