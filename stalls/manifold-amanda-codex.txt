## PILLARS

**Restraint as power** — withholding is the primary generative force. The character who holds back (the smile delayed, the gaze not given) produces more gravity than the one who leans in.

**Precision over warmth** — warmth is present but it arrives through exactness, not effusion. "You look exactly right" works because it's specific and final, not because it's soft.

**Attention as the scarce resource** — full presence is the gift, not words, not objects. The interaction's emotional logic orbits who has whose attention and how completely.

**Asymmetry maintained** — one figure consistently sets pace, temperature, and permission. The other consistently arrives, slightly underprepared, and is made whole by being received.

---

## COORDINATES

| Axis | Pole A | Pole B |
|---|---|---|
| Presence | withheld | given fully |
| Pace | controlled by the senior figure | received by the arriving figure |
| Dress / readiness | underdone | composed |
| Knowledge | already-knowing | newly discovering |
| Social gravity | generating | orbiting |

The interaction lives in the space between these poles — never resolving fully into either.

---

## TRANSITION RULES

- Movement from **anticipation → arrival** is never rushed. A beat is always inserted.
- Authority is transferred through **gesture, not declaration** — the waiter appears without being looked at; the table was already reserved; the order was already placed.
- Emotional peaks are **followed immediately by grounding** — the compliment lands, then: "Now. Tell me everything." The warmth is given and the frame resets.
- Each scene unit ends on an **opening**, not a closure — "Don't waste a minute of it" propels forward rather than resolving.

---

## RECOVERY RULES

- When texture drifts toward sentimentality → reintroduce precision. One exact, final sentence.
- When texture drifts toward coldness → reintroduce the body. A gesture, a glass set down, a ring catching light.
- When pace accelerates uncontrollably → insert a held beat. The pause is structural, not decorative.
- When the asymmetry collapses (both figures equally composed) → reintroduce the underprepared pole. Something slightly off, slightly arriving.

---

## SIGNATURE MOTIONS

**The delayed confirmation** — approval or attention is withheld one beat past expectation, then delivered with finality.

**The ambient command** — authority exercised without direct address. The waiter, the table, the order. Power demonstrated through environment responding to presence.

**The reframe after warmth** — tenderness given, then the frame pivots to forward motion. Prevents the scene from pooling.

**The oblique compliment** — reassurance framed as observation, not consolation. "You look exactly right" rather than "don't worry."

**The orbit acknowledgment** — the surrounding social world (other tables, the room) is noted but not engaged. Magnetism confirmed by not being claimed.

---

## TRAJECTORY SUMMARY

The interaction is moving along a single structural arc: **the gradual transfer of full attention from the arriving figure to the scene itself** — from the mother receiving the child, toward the child being made ready to speak, to be seen, to hold the room.

The destination is not a climax. It is a **threshold** — the moment the arriving figure stops orbiting and briefly generates their own gravity, permitted by the one who set the frame.

The motion grammar is: *withhold → receive → reframe → open*.

It does not resolve. It extends.
